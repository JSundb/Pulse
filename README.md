# Roamy

Roamy is a dynamic and intuitive application designed to help you discover local activities and connect with people who share your interests. Whether you're looking for a quiet café to study, a lively park for a workout, or a hidden gem to explore, Roamy provides tailored suggestions based on your mood and preferences.

## Features

-   **Activity Discovery:** Find activities that match your mood and interests, available right now or in the near future.
-   **Vibe Matching:** Discover places that fit your desired atmosphere—from quiet and chill to active and social.
-   **Social Connections:** Optionally, connect with like-minded individuals and join micro-groups centered around your favorite activities.
-   **Interest-Based Filtering:** Easily filter activities by categories such as Social, Fitness, Study, Explore, Deals, and Nightlife.
-   **Interactive Radar:** Visualize trending activities around you with an engaging and interactive radar screen.
-   **Customizable Profiles:** Personalize your experience by setting your interests, preferred energy levels, and social settings.

## Running the code

Run `npm i` to install the dependencies.

Run `npm run dev` to start the development server.
